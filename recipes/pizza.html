Pizza recipes

Pizza is one of my favorite recipes, so today i want to share it with you guys how to make a pizza in less than 15 minutes

    Ingredients
  • Pizza dough, OR you can just buy some instant pizza bottom
  • Chese: Mozzarella is a must,but you can have it with other one like cheedar,parmansan,...
  • Ketchup:Just some cheap ketchup is find, but i suggest you buy type that made for pizza because it have some orgareno leafs inside already
  • Topping: Just anything you want to add
    Step how to make
  1. put your dought or pizza pan in a sauce pan for about 5 minutes in small heat. This will make your pizza later be more crispy
  2. After about 5 mintues you will start to add topping.MAlways ketchup first, then the chese, and finally, toppings
  3. Put a cover over your pizza and set fire to medium heat about 10 minutes
  4. when the chese is melt is the time the meal is ready to serve
